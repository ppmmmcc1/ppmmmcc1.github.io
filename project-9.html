SENTINEL ENVIRONMENTAL SENSOR

IOT | SOLAR | ENVIRONMENTAL MONITORING

CONCEPT OVERVIEW

Most environmental monitoring is either low tech and very local, or advanced and too expensive for wide use. My concept is a low cost, solar powered sensor that can run on its own while collecting high value climate data.

TECHNICAL APPROACH

Each one would measure CO₂, particulate matter, temperature, humidity, and other pollutants, sending the data securely to a decentralized network. This would let communities, researchers, and policy makers see real time environmental maps without relying on a small number of expensive monitoring stations.

IMPACT VISION

Widespread deployment would create a comprehensive environmental monitoring network, providing critical data for climate research and public health initiatives at unprecedented scale and resolution.
