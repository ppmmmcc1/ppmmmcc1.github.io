AUTOMATED SOLAR FARM DRONES

AI | ROBOTICS | RENEWABLE ENERGY

CONCEPT OVERVIEW

Building and maintaining solar farms, especially in remote areas, takes a lot of time and money. My concept is a coordinated swarm of AI driven worker drones that can transport, position, and install solar panels with very little human help.

TECHNICAL APPROACH

Each drone would have a role, some for ground prep, others for carrying panels, and others for precision placement. A larger queen drone would act as a mobile base station with extra power and tools for the swarm. The idea is a self deploying system that could set up solar installations in challenging terrain where traditional construction equipment can't operate efficiently.

IMPACT VISION

This would dramatically reduce the cost and time required to deploy renewable energy infrastructure, making solar power viable in previously inaccessible locations.
