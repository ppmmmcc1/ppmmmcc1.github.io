HYDROPONIC EC AUTOMATION

ARDUINO | SENSORS | WATER

Part of my work toward a Type I civilization. Read the essay →

CONCEPT OVERVIEW

Hydroponics lives and dies on precision. If your nutrient levels are off your plants either stall or burn. Manually checking EC and dosing nutrients gets old fast, especially when you have multiple systems running. My concept is an Arduino based automation unit that handles it all, EC sensing, pH tracking, and nutrient dosing through a set of small pumps.

TECHNICAL APPROACH

The probes would be modular for easy swap outs, and all electronics would sit in a sealed enclosure to survive high humidity. I want it to learn your system's patterns over time, adjusting dosing schedules based on plant growth stages and environmental changes. The goal is set it and forget it automation that actually improves crop yields.

IMPACT VISION

This would make precision agriculture accessible to small scale growers, reducing crop loss from nutrient imbalances while freeing up time for other critical farming tasks.
