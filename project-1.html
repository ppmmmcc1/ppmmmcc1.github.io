FILAMENT RECYCLER

3D PRINTING | SUSTAINABILITY

CONCEPT OVERVIEW

3D printing is great until you see the pile of wasted plastic, failed prints, support material, leftover spools that can't be reused without the right gear. I want to flip that problem. This concept is a beginner friendly, low maintenance filament recycler that works for any material from basic PLA to abrasive carbon blends.

TECHNICAL APPROACH

I would design a rugged metal hopper that can handle heavy use, feed it into a hardened shredder, and push the material through a precisely heated extrusion system. The key is making it simple enough for makers to operate but robust enough to handle different plastics without constant maintenance or expensive replacements.

IMPACT VISION

This would turn 3D printing waste from a disposal problem into a resource loop, making additive manufacturing more sustainable and cost-effective for both hobbyists and production environments.
